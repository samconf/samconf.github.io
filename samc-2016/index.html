Highlights

  • Over 150 registered attendees, including expert panelists, industry leaders, and social justice activists from across the world
  • 11 sponsors including TV Asia, New York Life, India.com, Brown Girl Magazine, and many more
  • Keynote addresses from Shashi Tharoor, Amna Nawaz, and Sonia Katyal
  • Performances by Ramya Ramana and Nadia P. Manzoor
  • Workshops
    • “Does Bollywood Perpetuate Rape Culture?”
    • “Post 9/11 Climate: Islamophobia, Policing, and Pathway”
  • Panels
    • “Challenging the Engineer, Doctor, Lawyer Stereotype”
    • “Maternal Health and Reproductive Rights in Pakistan”
    • “An Inquiry into Sexual and Domestic Violence in South Asia”
    • “My Art is My Activism”
    • “Transgender Rights in South Asia”
  • Bonding events such as an open-mic night, a networking lunch, and an after party for conference attendees
